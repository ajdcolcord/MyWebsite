About Me

Throughout high school and into college, I have always been extremely interested in new technologies and innovations. With my studies at Northeastern and my Co-op experiences, I have the goal to learn as much as I can to become a strong Software Developer.

I have had the opportunity to be involved with IT for quite a few years, specifically with my work at Brewster Academy. Throughout my time there, I grew my desire to have an impact on people by connecting with them and helping them. I was able to learn enterprise-level data warehousing technologies such as Teradata and Informatica, as well as growing my foundation in UNIX and experimenting with Big Data applications.
